// ABOUT

THE BRIDGE

// BIO

Art. Code.
The space between.

I'm Taha — a technical artist and designer who operates in the gap most teams struggle to close. I speak the language of artists and the language of engineers, and I use both to turn creative vision into something real, playable, and felt.

Across game design, environment art, level design, and engine-side technical work, I've built the kind of breadth that makes teams move faster and produce better work. When you understand a concept artist's intent and the constraints of the renderer executing it — when you can design the space and script the systems living inside it — you stop being one piece of the pipeline and start being the person who holds it together.

DEV LOG
2025 · UE5
Project Anomaly begins as an Unreal Engine 5 prototype. Core FPS movement, combat loop, and early level blockouts are established. The boomer shooter direction — fast, aggressive, punishing — is locked in.
2025 · PORT
Mid-prototype, the project is ported to Godot 4. Core systems are rebuilt in GDScript, enabling faster iteration and full engine ownership. Unreal prototype footage preserved as development reference.
2026 · ACTIVE
Active development continues in Godot 4. Steam page is live — wishlist open. Progress updates dropping regularly.
